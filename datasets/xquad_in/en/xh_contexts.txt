NgoMeyi 20, 2009, uMay kunye noTaylor badlale ingoma ethi "Sizintshatsheli" bephila kumdlalo wokugqibela weAmerican Idol apho ophumelele uKris Allen kunye nembaleki uAdam Lambert benikezela ngengoma. Embindini ka-2009, emva kokwahlulwa kukaKumkanikazi + uPaul Rodgers, iwebhusayithi ye-Queen ekwi-intanethi yabhengeza eyona nto intsha idumileyo ebizwa ngokuba yi-Absolute Greatest. Icwecwe likhutshwe nge-16 kaNovemba kwaye liphezulu kwinombolo ye-3 kwiTshati yase-UK esemthethweni. Icwecwe liqulethe ama-20 eengoma ezinkulu zika-Queen ezithatha umsebenzi wabo wonke kwaye yakhululwa ngefomathi ezine ezahlukeneyo: idiski enye, idiski ephindwe kabini (eneenkcazo), idiski ephindwe kabini enencwadi yenqaku, kunye nerekhodi yevinyl. Ngaphambi kokuba ikhutshwe, ukhuphiswano lwaluqhutywa nguKumkanikazi kwi-intanethi ukuqikelela uluhlu lweengoma njengentengiso yecwecwe.

Ukususela kwimbongo yasePersi eyayiyiphefumlele kakhulu, izuze ubutyebi beesimboli ezineentsingiselo kunye nobudlelwane-zombini ukufana (مراعات نزیر mura'ât-i nazîr / تانس تناسب) kunye nenkcaso (تضاد tezâd) yayingaphezulu okanye ingaphantsi emiselweyo. Umbongo weDivan waqanjwa ngokudityaniswa okuthe gqolo kwemifanekiso emininzi enjalo ngaphakathi kwesakhelo esingqongqo semetriki, ngaloo ndlela ivumela iintsingiselo ezininzi ezinokuvela ukuba zivele. Uninzi lwemibongo yeDivan yayinomculo ngokwendalo: nokuba ziigazeli (ezenza eyona nxalenye inkulu yerepertoire yesiko), okanye iikasîdes. Kwakukho, nangona kunjalo, ezinye iindidi eziqhelekileyo, ngakumbi i-mesnevî, uhlobo lwevesi yothando kunye noluhlu lwemibongo ebalisayo; eyona mizekelo mibini iphawulekayo yale fomu yiLeyli kunye neMajnun yaseFuzûlî kunye neHüsn ü Aşk yaseŞeyh Gâlib.

Ngomhla we-15 ku-Agasti 2006, uBrian May waqinisekisa ngewebhusayithi yakhe kunye neklabhu yabalandeli ukuba uKumkanikazi + uPaul Rodgers uza kuqalisa ukuvelisa i-albhamu yabo yokuqala ye-studio eqala ngo-Okthobha, ukuba irekhodwe "kwindawo eyimfihlo". UKumkanikazi + uPaul Rodgers wadlala kuMbhiyozo weminyaka engama-90 yokuzalwa kukaNelson Mandela owawubanjelwe eHyde Park, eLondon ngomhla wama-27 kuJuni ka-2008, ukukhumbula iminyaka engamashumi alithoba yokuzalwa kukaMandela, kwaye kwakhona ukukhuthaza ukwazisa ngobhubhane we-HIV/AIDS. I-albhamu yokuqala kaKumkanikazi + uPaul Rodgers, enesihloko esithi The Cosmos Rocks, yakhululwa eYurophu ngomhla we-12 kuSeptemba 2008 kwaye e-United States nge-28 ka-Oktobha 2008. Emva kokukhululwa kwe-albhamu, iqela laphinda lahamba nge-tour ngeYurophu, livula kwi-Kharkiv's Freedom Square phambi kwabalandeli be-350,000 baseUkraine. Ikonsathi yaseKharkiv kamva yakhululwa kwiDVD. Ukhenketho emva koko lwafudukela eRussia, kwaye iqela lenze imiboniso emibini ethengisiweyo eMoscow Arena. Ukugqiba umlenze wokuqala wokhenketho olubanzi lwaseYurophu, olubone iqela lidlala iintsuku ezili-15 ezithengisiweyo kumazwe alithoba, umlenze wase-UK wokhenketho wathengisa ngaphakathi kwemizuzu engama-90 yokuthengisa kwaye wabandakanya neentsuku ezintathu zaseLondon, eyokuqala yayiyi-O2 nge-13 ka-Okthobha. Umlenze wokugqibela wokhenketho wenziwa eMzantsi Melika, kwaye wawuquka ikhonsathi ethengisiweyo kwi-Estadio José Amalfitani, eBuenos Aires.

Umatshini wokuLinga woMgangatho omncinci waseManchester, obizwa ngokuba nguBaby, yayiyeyokuqala inkqubo yekhompyutha egciniweyo yehlabathi. Yakhiwa kwiYunivesithi yaseVictoria yaseManchester nguFrederic C. Williams, uTom Kilburn kunye noGeoff Tootill, kwaye baqhuba inkqubo yokuqala ngomhla we-21 Juni 1948. Yenzelwe njengovavanyo lwe-tube ye-Williams, i-random-access digital storage device yokuqala. Nangona ikhomputha yayithathwa ngokuba "incinci kwaye yamandulo" ngemigangatho yexesha layo, yayingumatshini wokuqala osebenzayo ukuba uqulethe zonke izinto ezibalulekileyo kwikhompyutheni yanamhlanje. Ngokukhawuleza ukuba i-SSEM ibonise ukuba nokwenzeka koyilo lwayo, iprojekthi yaqalwa eyunivesithi ukuyiphuhlisa ibe yikhompyutheni esebenzisekayo ngakumbi, iManchester Mark 1.

Oku kwaphumela ekubeni inkcubeko nolwimi lwamaGrike zithunyelwe kumazwe angaphandle kwezi ndawo zintsha ngamathanga amaGrike aseMakedoni, aqala ukuya kuthi ga kwiPakistan yanamhlanje. Noko ke, ngokufanayo, ezi zikumkani zitsha zaphenjelelwa ziinkcubeko zomthonyama, zamkela izenzo zasekuhlaleni apho ziluncedo, ziyimfuneko, okanye zifanelekile. Inkcubeko yamaGrike ke ngoko imele ukudityaniswa kwehlabathi laMandulo lamaGrike kunye nelaseMpuma Ekufuphi, kuMbindi Mpuma, kunye nakumaZantsi-ntshona eAsia, kunye nokusuka kwisimo sengqondo sangaphambili samaGrike kwiinkcubeko "zamabhari". Ixesha lamaGrike laliphawulwa liza elitsha lobukoloniyali bamaGrike (njengoko lahluliwe kwelo lalisenzeka ngenkulungwane yesi-8 ukuya kweyesi-6 BC) elathi laseka izixeko nezikumkani zamaGrike eAsia naseAfrika. Ezi zixeko zitsha zenziwe ngabakholoni bamaGrike abavela kwiindawo ezahlukeneyo zehlabathi lamaGrike, kwaye kungekhona, njengangaphambili, ukusuka "kwisixeko sikamama". Amaziko aphambili enkcubeko anda ukusuka kwilizwe laseGrisi ukuya ePergamon, eRhodes, nakwiikoloni ezintsha zamaGrike ezinjengeSeleucia, iAntiyokwe, iAlexandria kunye neAi-Khanoum. Lo mxube wabantu abathetha isiGrike wavelisa ulwimi lwezizwana oluqhelekileyo olusekelwe eAttic, olwalubizwa ngokuba sisiGrike saseKoine, esathi saba lulwimi oluthethwa kakhulu kulo lonke ihlabathi lamaGrike.

Ekuqaleni kwenkulungwane ye-19 eJamani iRhineland naseMazantsi eNetherlands, isithethe samaxesha aphakathi esibuthathaka saphinda saphinda saphinda savela. Ngokuqhubekayo ngenkulungwane ye-18 neye-19 yeXesha Eliqhelekileyo, njengenxalenye yokuxhatshazwa kwaminyaka le kweSaturnalia kwi-carnival yaseRoma, oorabhi babenyanzelwa ukuba benze umngcelele kwizitrato zesixeko benxibe isidima sobudenge, begculelwa yaye bephoswa yimijukujelwa eyahlukeneyo yesihlwele. Isibongozo samaYuda aseRoma esathunyelwa ngowe-1836 kuPopu uGregory XVI ukuba aphelise ukuxhatshazwa kwaminyaka le nxamnye namaSemite Saturnalia sachaswa: “Asilothuba lifanelekileyo ukwenza naluphi na uhlobo lwentshatsheli.”
Ezinye zezona zithethe zaziwa kakhulu, kuquka imiboniso yenyama kunye neebhola zobuso, zaqala ukurekhodwa kwi-Italian yaphakathi. I-carnival yaseVenice, ixesha elide, yayiyeyona carnival idumileyo (nangona uNapoleon wayibhangisa ngo-1797 kwaye kuphela ngo-1979 isithethe sabuyiselwa).
